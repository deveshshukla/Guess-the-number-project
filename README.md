## 🕹️ About the Game

**Guess My Number** is a fun and interactive **number-guessing puzzle game** built using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.  
The player’s goal is to correctly guess a hidden number within a specific range — depending on the difficulty level chosen.

Each wrong guess reduces your score, and the challenge increases with higher levels.  
Your **highest score is saved separately for each level** so you can compete with yourself!

🎮 **Play it live:** [Guess My Number on GitHub Pages](https://deveshshukla.github.io/Guess-the-number-project/)

---

## 🌟 Features

- 🧩 Three difficulty levels:
  - **Easy:** Range 1–20 | Score 20 | Penalty −1
  - **Medium:** Range 1–30 | Score 30 | Penalty −2
  - **Hard:** Range 1–30 | Score 30 | Penalty −3
- 🧠 Level-specific high score tracking  
- ⚡ Instant feedback on guesses  
- 🎨 Clean and responsive UI  
- 🔊 Optional sound effects (if added later)  
- 🚫 No login or data storage — one-time quick play

---

## 🕹️ How to Play

1. Choose your preferred **difficulty level** (Easy, Medium, or Hard).  
2. Enter your guess using the **keyboard**.  
3. Click the **"Check"** button or press Enter to see if you guessed correctly.  
4. Each wrong guess reduces your score based on the level penalty.  
5. Try to get the **highest score** before your points hit zero!
